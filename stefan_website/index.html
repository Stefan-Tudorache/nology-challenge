I come from a very simple background, where technology was scarce or belittle. However this didn't stop my love for computers to grow and assimilate every little thing along the way. Now i feel prepared to go even further and make a dream come true for me and my loved ones.

First Step

First you have to learn to walk before you run and so i started my Html and Css course. Seeing what i could achieve with just a few lines of code and how natural, easy and self explanatory is, it just heightened my desire of going forward to discover more of this so far hidden world.

The Challenge

No journey comes without its challenges and so JavaScript slapt my brain out of the dormant state it used to be and put it to the test. While logic is logic, learning how to put together the syntax to give it meaning seemed a bit daunting at first. One thing was clear though, the power behind every line and what you could achieve with it was greater than everything i've seen before.

Not Alone
